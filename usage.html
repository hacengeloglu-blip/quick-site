How usage works.

Last updated: 2026-05-13

QuickAI uses a weekly token budget. No daily resets, no waiting, no cooldowns between answers.

Your weekly cap

Every plan gets a weekly token budget that resets on a rolling seven-day window. There is no fixed reset day, just a clean seven-day clock from your first answer of the cycle.

  • Free. 5,000 tokens / week
  • Pro. 50,000 tokens / week · 10× Free
  • Max. 100,000 tokens / week · 20× Free

A token is roughly three-quarters of a word. A short answer might use a few hundred tokens; a long, detailed one a few thousand. You can see real-time usage in the app under Settings › Usage.

What happens at the limit

Speed stays the same from 0% to 100%. At 80%, a heads-up banner appears so you can pace yourself or upgrade. At 100%, new requests are blocked until your cap resets. Any answer already streaming finishes normally. No model swap, no throttle, no cooldown. The number is the meter, and we tell you the truth about it.

Why tokens

Tokens are how every modern AI model actually measures work, bits of words and punctuation that map directly to compute cost. We show you the same number we use to plan capacity, so what you see is the truth, not a marketing abstraction.

Reset cadence

Your week is a rolling seven-day window. If you used 12,000 tokens last Monday, that bucket frees up next Monday. No waiting until the first of the month.
