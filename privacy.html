Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-13

Who we are

QuickAI is made by Hookson Studio. Questions? Email support@hooksonstudio.com.

What we collect

The minimum needed to run the service:

  • Account identity. When you sign in with Apple, we receive an anonymised user ID and, optionally, your name and email. We store the ID and a display name in our database. We never see your Apple ID credentials.
  • Your questions and answers. Each thread (your question and the generated answer) is stored in our database linked to your account so you can review history.
  • Subscription status. If you purchase Pro or Max, RevenueCat (our payment processor) sends us a record of your entitlement. We store subscription state to enable paid features. RevenueCat processes payments; we never touch your payment card details.
  • Usage data. We record when you sent a question, the approximate token count, and whether the request succeeded. This powers your weekly cap and lets us diagnose problems.
  • Crash reports. Firebase Crashlytics collects crash traces if the app crashes. No question content is included.

What we do not collect

  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not build advertising profiles.
  • We do not sell or rent your data to any third party.
  • We do not read your clipboard beyond what you paste into the Ask field.

Service providers

We share data with these providers solely to operate the service:

  • Google Firebase. Authentication, database, crash reporting.
  • Firebase App Check. Verifies requests come from a genuine copy of QuickAI to prevent abuse.
  • Google Gemini. Your questions are sent to Gemini to generate answers. Google's data-use policies apply to request content.
  • RevenueCat. Subscription management and receipt verification.
  • Cloudflare. Our backend runs on Cloudflare Workers. Cloudflare may log request metadata (IP, timestamp) per their standard policies.

Data retention and deletion

Your threads stay in your account until you delete them. You can delete any thread from the app's history view. Deleting a thread removes both the question and the answer from our database. To delete your entire account and all associated data, use Settings › Delete account in the app, or email support@hooksonstudio.com.

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication uses Sign in with Apple with short-lived tokens. We do not store passwords. Firestore security rules restrict each user to their own data.

Children

QuickAI is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Email support@hooksonstudio.com with any privacy questions or requests.
