How usage works.

Quick uses a weekly token budget. No daily resets, no waiting, no cooldowns between answers.

Your weekly cap

Every plan gets a generous weekly token budget that resets every seven days on a rolling window — there's no fixed reset day, just a clean seven-day clock from your first answer of the cycle.

  • Free — 15,000 tokens / week
  • Pro — 75,000 tokens / week
  • Max — 300,000 tokens / week

A token is roughly three-quarters of a word. A short answer might use a few hundred tokens; a long, detailed one might use a few thousand. You'll see your real-time usage in the app under Settings → Usage.

What happens at the limit

Below your cap: full speed.

Approaching your cap (above 80%): a small in-app banner reminds you.

At your cap: Quick switches to a faster, lighter model so you can keep going. You don't get blocked mid-answer — ever.

This is a soft cap, not a hard wall. Most people never see it.

Fair use

Max gets a generous 300,000-tokens-per-week cap because most users land closer to a third of that. Sustained heavy use beyond the cap may be throttled to keep the service fast for everyone. We'll never bill you a surprise fee — at worst, your speed drops slightly until the week resets.

Why tokens

Tokens are how every modern AI model actually measures work — bits of words and punctuation that map directly to compute cost. We show you the same number we use to plan capacity, so what you see is the truth, not a marketing abstraction.

Reset cadence

Your week is a rolling seven-day window. If you used 12,000 tokens last Monday, that bucket frees up next Monday — no waiting until the first of the month.

You can see your current usage anytime in the Quick app, under Settings → Usage.
